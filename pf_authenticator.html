PlasmoFAST: Plasmodium Fast Authenticator of Strains

Paper

PlasmoFAST is a fast, kmer-based, strain identification tool built developed to identify Plasmodium falciparum lab strains NF54, 3D7, 7G8, W2, Dd2, D10, HB3, and GB4 from sequencing data. The tool has been benchmarked to work with a variety of genomic material (WGS, RNA-seq, and ChIP-seq) and sequencing technologies (Illumina short reads, and Pacbio Sequel, ONT MinION and ONT Promethion long reads). PlasmoFAST can also detect mixed/contaminated samples and is species specific to Plasmodium falciparum.
